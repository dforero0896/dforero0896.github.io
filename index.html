About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Institute of Cosmos Sciences (ICCUB) of the University of Barcelona. My research focuses on large-scale structure cosmology using data from spectroscopic surveys like DESI. I develop and apply fast mock simulations, covariance estimation methods, machine learning techniques, and novel clustering statistics to extract robust cosmological constraints.

I earned my PhD in Physics from EPFL (Switzerland), where I worked on the sensitivity of void baryon acoustic oscillations to selection effects and created simulation-based models for the cosmic web. Before that, I completed a double BSc in Physics and Geoscience in Colombia.
